Max Healthcare

Max Healthcare is one of India’s leading hospital chains, known for its comprehensive healthcare services, cutting-edge technology, and emphasis on patient-centered care. Based in New Delhi, Max Healthcare operates hospitals across India, primarily in North India, and offers advanced treatment options in various specialties.

UnitedHealth Group

UnitedHealth Group is a leading American health and well-being company and one of the largest healthcare organizations globally. Headquartered in Minnetonka, Minnesota, UnitedHealth Group operates through two main business divisions: UnitedHealthcare, which focuses on health benefits and insurance, and Optum.

Fortis Healthcare

Fortis Healthcare is another major private healthcare provider in India, known for its high-quality medical care, specialty services, and patient-centered approach. Founded in 2001, Fortis has grown into a network of hospitals.

Apollo Hospitals

Apollo Hospitals is one of India's largest and most renowned healthcare providers, known for its advanced medical facilities, comprehensive healthcare services, and cutting-edge technology. Established in 1983 by Dr. Prathap C. Reddy.

Abbott Healthcare

Abbott is a global healthcare company that operates in multiple sectors, including diagnostics, medical devices, nutrition, and branded generic pharmaceuticals. Founded in 1888 and headquartered in the United States, Abbott has established itself as a leader in developing innovative healthcare solutions to improve health outcomes.

Manipal Hospital

Manipal Hospitals is one of India's largest and most trusted healthcare providers, known for its patient-centric care, advanced medical technology, and comprehensive services. Established in 1953 as part of the Manipal Education and Medical Group (MEMG), Manipal Hospitals has grown significantly and operates a network of hospitals across India and internationally, with locations in countries like Malaysia and the UAE.

Narayan Health

Narayana Health is one of India’s largest and most prominent healthcare providers, particularly known for its high-quality, affordable healthcare services. Founded in 2000 by renowned cardiac surgeon Dr. Devi Prasad Shetty, Narayana Health (NH) operates with a mission to make world-class healthcare accessible to all.

CVS health

CVS Health is a leading American healthcare company, known for its expansive pharmacy network, health insurance services, and commitment to accessible and affordable healthcare solutions. Headquartered in Woonsocket, Rhode Island, CVS Health operates through several divisions, including its retail pharmacy chain, pharmacy benefit management (PBM), and the health insurance provider Aetna.

Meera Bnagles RAJKOT

Meera Bangles is a well-known brand based in Rajkot, Gujarat, specializing in the manufacturing and retail of high-quality bangles. Rajkot is famous for its exquisite craftsmanship in traditional jewelry, and Meera Bangles has made a significant mark in the bangle industry by offering a wide range of designs that cater to various tastes, from traditional to contemporary.
